Our Story

OreoStudios is not just a development studio — it’s the result of passion, curiosity, and years of learning by building.

Hi, I’m Elias 👋

Hi! I’m Elias, a very young developer and the founder of OreoStudios. Our studio has a story — and it didn’t start with money, investors, or big companies.

It all started when I was 11 years old. I had a Minecraft server and a lot of ideas, but I didn’t have enough money to pay developers. So instead of giving up, I decided to learn everything myself.

I started with simple plugins, then more complex systems. Year after year, I evolved — learning Java, backend systems, infrastructure, databases, networking, and game design.

What began as a hobby slowly became a real skillset. And at some point, I realized: this could be more than just passion.

From Freelance to Studio

Over the years, I worked with and for several development services and studios, gaining professional experience and understanding how real production environments work.

  • Development services & freelance platforms
  • Game systems, plugins, backend infrastructure
  • Client communication, deadlines, and quality standards

After growing both technically and professionally, my team and I made a decision: create our own studio.

That decision is how OreoStudios came to life.
