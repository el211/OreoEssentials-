🎅 Santa Event

Santa spawns in configurable regions and distributes rewards to nearby players.

Configuration (santa.yml)

santa:
  enabled: true
  spawn:
    world: world
    region: "spawn_santa"
  rewards:
    commands:
      - "eco give %player% 500"
      - "shards give %player% 25"
    message: "&aSanta has visited and rewarded you!"
